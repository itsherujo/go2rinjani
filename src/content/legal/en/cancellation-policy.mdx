### Additional Terms

- Refunds for the remaining balance (where applicable) will be processed within **14 business days** of the written cancellation acknowledgment.
- Refunds will be returned via the original payment method. Bank transfer fees and currency conversion losses are borne by the client.
- The effective cancellation date is the date on which Go2Rinjani **receives and acknowledges** the cancellation in writing — not the date the client sends the request.

## Force Majeure & Cancellation by Go2Rinjani

Go2Rinjani may cancel or suspend a tour due to circumstances beyond our reasonable control ("Force Majeure"), including but not limited to:

- Elevated volcanic activity on Mount Rinjani (Alert Level II or above as declared by PVMBG)
- Official park closure ordered by TNGR or the Indonesian government
- Extreme weather conditions rendering the trek unsafe (as determined by our guide team and/or relevant meteorological authority)
- Natural disasters including earthquake, flood, or landslide
- Public health emergencies declared by national or regional authorities

<Callout type="info">
  **Client Protection:** In the event of cancellation by Go2Rinjani due to Force Majeure, the client is entitled to a **full refund of all amounts paid, including the 20% deposit**. Refunds will be processed within 14 business days.
</Callout>

Alternatively, if the client prefers, Go2Rinjani will offer a **free reschedule** to a future available date of equivalent package value, valid for up to **12 months** from the original departure date.

<p class="text-mono mt-8 text-center text-xs">╌╌╌╌</p>

## Tour Interruption (Early Departure)

If a client voluntarily chooses to leave the trek before its completion for any personal reason (including but not limited to injury caused by personal negligence, fatigue, or change of mind):

- No partial refund will be issued for unused tour days or services.
- Go2Rinjani will make reasonable efforts to assist with safe descent and logistics, at cost to the client.
- If early departure is due to a medical emergency, our team will coordinate emergency assistance; costs may be covered by personal travel insurance.

<p class="text-mono mt-8 text-center text-xs">╌╌╌╌</p>

## Rescheduling

- Clients may request **one (1) complimentary reschedule** if the request is made at least **14 days** before the original departure date.
- Subsequent reschedule requests will incur an administrative fee of **IDR 250,000** per request.
- Rescheduled dates are subject to availability and must be confirmed in writing by Go2Rinjani.
- Rescheduling does not apply to the deposit, which remains non-refundable.
- Price differences arising from peak season rescheduling will be charged to the client.
