## Booking & Reservation

- A booking is considered confirmed only upon receipt of a **20% non-refundable deposit** of the total package price.
- The remaining **80% balance** must be paid in full upon arrival at our designated meeting point, prior to the commencement of the tour.
- Bookings are subject to availability. Go2Rinjani reserves the right to decline any reservation at its discretion.
- A booking confirmation will be sent within **24 hours** of successful deposit payment.
- All bookings must include accurate personal information: full legal name (as per passport/ID), date of birth, nationality, emergency contact, and any relevant medical conditions.

## Package Inclusions & Exclusions

### Included in Your Package

- Return land transportation from agreed pick-up point to the trailhead
- Licensed, certified, and experienced professional guide(s)
- Porter service (standard weight allocation per person)
- All meals during the trek as specified in the itinerary
- Camping equipment: tent, sleeping bag, trekking poles, and cooking gear
- National Park entrance fee and porter/guide insurance (BPJS)
- Simaksi (official trekking permit issued by TNGR)

### Not Included

- Personal travel insurance *(strongly recommended)*
- Personal trekking gear (boots, clothing, personal medication)
- Alcoholic beverages and personal expenses
- Gratuities/tips for guides and porters *(discretionary)*
- Emergency evacuation costs not covered by personal insurance

## Health, Fitness & Medical Requirements

Mount Rinjani (3,726 m / 12,224 ft) is a strenuous high-altitude trek requiring a good level of physical fitness. By confirming your booking, you declare that:

- You are in good physical health and fit for high-altitude trekking activities.
- You do not have any medical condition that would put yourself or others at risk during the trek.
- You have disclosed any pre-existing medical conditions to Go2Rinjani in writing prior to departure.
- You accept full personal responsibility for your physical condition during the tour.

<Callout type="warning">
  **Important:** Go2Rinjani reserves the right to refuse participation or remove any client from the trek if our guide reasonably believes their health or conduct poses a safety risk to themselves or the group. No refund will be issued in such circumstances.
</Callout>

## Client Responsibilities & Code of Conduct

- Clients must follow all instructions given by the assigned guide at all times during the trek.
- Clients must respect the natural environment and adhere to **Leave No Trace** principles.
- The collection, removal, or damage of any flora, fauna, rocks, or cultural artifacts within the National Park is strictly prohibited.
- Open fires outside designated areas are prohibited.
- Clients are required to carry their own valid government-issued ID/passport for park entry verification.
- Any behavior deemed disrespectful toward guides, porters, local communities, or fellow trekkers will result in immediate removal from the tour without refund.

Go2Rinjani reserves the right to modify or alter any itinerary at any time before or during the tour. Modifications may be necessary due to:
