Whether you are looking for a short 2-day hike or a full 4-day summit and lake experience, preparing for Mount Rinjani is essential. We've got you covered with all the tips you need.

Co-trekking spots are available during the dry season from April to December. Contact us for more information on the best trekking packages.

## Trekking Packages

**Senaru to Crater Rim (2 Days, 1 Night)**
Perfect for those short on time. Experience the breathtaking views of the crater lake and the Segara Anak volcano.

**Sembalun to Summit (3 Days, 2 Nights)**
The classic Rinjani challenge. Push your limits and reach the 3,726m summit for sunrise.

**Advanced Explorer (4 Days, 3 Nights)**
The ultimate Rinjani experience. Includes the summit, the lake, and the hot springs.

## Essential Gear

- Sturdy trekking boots
- Warm clothing for the summit (it gets below freezing!)
- Headlamp
- Trekking poles
- Sun protection (hat, sunscreen, sunglasses)
